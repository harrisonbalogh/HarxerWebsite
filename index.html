    HXNotes

    Mac OS X

            HXNotes is essentially a college assistant. It rolls the functionality of a calendar, scheduler, agenda, and note taker into one. Upon filling in a semester's schedule, the app will automatically populate its notebook as the semester goes along. It can remind you of work as well as exams - with optional alerts. Knowing your schedule allows you to select lectures as due dates rather than ambigous calendar dates. It will also note when the application was not opened during a lecture period and use this to keep track of lectures that were note attended. This is something I want to take beyond just being a project and eventually publish it to the Mac App Store. I currently use this app and will distribute it to others for testing soon. This is still very much under development.
